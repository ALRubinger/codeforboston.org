Challenge Statements

  • Resilient Coders: How might we enable better communication between parole officers and parolees?
  • Dudley Street Neighborhood Initiative: How might we help students show that they were late to school due to transit delays?
  • Boston Department of Youth Engagement & Employment: How might we help young people better prepare for a career by helping them understand the impact of their online presence and social media usage?
  • Boston Department of Youth Engagement & Employment: How might we help students to show off the courses and workshops they have taken from local non-profits to potential employers and each other?
  • Youth Hub Boston: How might we tap into and leverage the existing youth social networks to promote trusted resources that help youth achieve their educational and career goals?
  • Have your own idea? Fill out the CodeAcross 2016 pitch form. We'll take a look and connect with you the morning of the event!
